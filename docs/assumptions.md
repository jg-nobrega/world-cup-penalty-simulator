# Assumptions

## Knockout Stage Teams

The teams included in the knockout stage were selected based on personal predictions and football analysis.

The selection considered:

- Team strength
- Recent performance
- Squad quality
- Historical results in major tournaments

The objective is not to predict the actual FIFA World Cup outcome, but to create a realistic knockout stage scenario for penalty shootout simulations.

This dataset may be updated as the tournament progresses.



## Simulation Assumptions

- All knockout matches are assumed to end in a draw.
- Every knockout match is decided by a penalty shootout.
- Team strength will be calculated using penalty taker and goalkeeper statistics.
- Psychological, environmental and external factors are not considered.
